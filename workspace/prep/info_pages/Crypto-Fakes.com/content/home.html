Recent Scam Alert: Mobomo Impersonation Moves away from .xyz Domain

Scammers impersonating Mobomo have quickly shifted their operations to a new domain, after their .xyz site was exposed. Learn how to protect yourself from these evolving tactics.

Mobomo Impersonation Continues:

Just days after their mobomo.xyz domain was exposed, the scammers have moved to new domain, continuing their recruitment scam via WhatsApp. This highlights the persistence and adaptability of online scammers.

Mobomo's Official Warning:

Mobomo has issued a public warning about the impersonation scam on their official website. Here's a screenshot of the alert:

You can view the original alert here: Mobomo Official Website

Key Takeaways:

  • Scammers are quick to adapt and change domains.
  • Always verify job offers through official company channels.
  • Be wary of unsolicited recruitment messages on social media.
  • Double-check domain names for misspellings or unusual extensions (.xyz).
